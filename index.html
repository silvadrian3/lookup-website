Lookup is a cloud-based business management system designed for online sellers to organize products, track deliveries, create sales invoices and expense claims, manage customers and suppliers' information — all in one go.

It allows you to have an overview of your business and track cash movement any time of the day online.


Point of Sale and Cash Flow Management

Organize your sales and expenses, track cash movement, run reports - all in one place.


Deliveries and Inventory Management

Easily track your deliveries and monitor your inventory to know which products have to be restocked.


Customers, Suppliers, and Employees Management

Manage Customer and Suppliers transactions and track your Employees working hours.
